Trading Dictionary

A-Z Trading Glossary

200+ اصطلاحات — اردو اور انگریزی میں
Bull Market
A market trending upward with rising prices. Investors are optimistic.
بڑھتی ہوئی مارکیٹ
Bear Market
A market trending downward with falling prices. Negative sentiment dominates.
گرتی ہوئی مارکیٹ
Pip
Smallest price movement. For EUR/USD = 0.0001. For USD/JPY = 0.01.
قیمت کی سب سے چھوٹی تبدیلی
Leverage
Using broker's money to control larger positions. 1:100 means $1 controls $100.
قرض پر ٹریڈنگ
Spread
Difference between Buy (Ask) and Sell (Bid) price. This is broker's fee.
خرید اور فروخت کی قیمت کا فرق
Stop Loss (SL)
Automatic order to close trade at a loss limit to protect your account.
خودکار نقصان روکنے کا آرڈر
Take Profit (TP)
Automatic order to close trade and lock in profit at your target price.
منافع پر خودکار بند
Drawdown
Peak to trough decline in account balance. 20% = lost 20% of capital.
اکاؤنٹ بیلنس میں کمی
Liquidity
Ease of buying/selling without affecting price. High liquidity = tight spreads.
خرید و فروخت کی آسانی
Order Block
SMC concept — zone where large institutions placed their buy/sell orders.
بڑے اداروں کا آرڈر زون
Fair Value Gap
A price gap left by fast momentum. Price tends to return to fill this gap.
قیمت کا خلا جو پُر ہوتا ہے
Market Cap
Total value of all coins/shares. Price × Total Supply. Ranks crypto importance.
کسی کرنسی کی کل مالیت
